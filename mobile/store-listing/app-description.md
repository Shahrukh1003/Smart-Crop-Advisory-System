## Full Description

### English
Smart Crop Advisory is your intelligent farming companion, designed specifically for Indian farmers. Get personalized crop recommendations, detect pests and diseases instantly, track market prices, and receive weather-based alerts - all in your native language.

**Key Features:**

🌾 **Personalized Crop Recommendations**
Get AI-powered suggestions based on your soil type, location, and weather conditions. Know which crops will give you the best yield and returns.

🐛 **Instant Pest & Disease Detection**
Simply take a photo of your affected crop and get instant identification with treatment recommendations - both organic and chemical options.

📊 **Real-time Market Prices**
Track prices from nearby markets within 50km. Get selling recommendations and transportation cost estimates.

🌤️ **Weather Alerts & Forecasts**
Receive 7-day forecasts and timely alerts for heavy rain, frost, or heat waves. Plan your farming activities accordingly.

🎤 **Voice Commands**
Speak in Kannada, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, or English. The app understands your queries and responds in your language.

📱 **Works Offline**
Access your recommendations and data even without internet. Sync automatically when connected.

- हिंदी (Hindi)
- தமிழ் (Tamil)
- తెలుగు (Telugu)
- English

Download now and transform your farming with the power of AI!
