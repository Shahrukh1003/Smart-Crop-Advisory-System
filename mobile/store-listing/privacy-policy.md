# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Introduction

Smart Crop Advisory ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Phone number (for account registration)
- Name
- Location (district, state, GPS coordinates)
- Language preference

### Agricultural Data
- Land parcel details (area, soil type)
- Crop history and farming activities
- Soil test results
- Pest detection images

### Usage Data
- App feature usage
- Session duration
- Device information

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide personalized crop recommendations
- Analyze pest and disease images
- Display nearby market prices
- Send weather alerts relevant to your location
- Improve our AI models and services
- Communicate important updates

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
- Personal data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
- Authentication tokens are stored securely in device keychain/keystore
- We use AWS infrastructure with industry-standard security

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- Government agricultural departments (anonymized, aggregated data only)
- Research institutions (for improving agricultural practices)
- Service providers (weather APIs, market data providers)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of analytics

## Data Retention

- Account data: Retained until account deletion
- Crop history: Retained for 5 years for recommendation improvement
- Analytics data: Anonymized after 1 year

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
